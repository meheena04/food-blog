Mansoor dal

Mansoor dal

May 21, 2024

Prep: 30 mins

Cook: 20 mins

Yields:Serves 4 people

Ingredients

  • 01. Rinse the Lentils

    Wash the red lentils thoroughly under running water until the water runs clear. Drain.

  • 02. Cook the Lentils

    In a large pot, add the rinsed lentils, water, turmeric powder, and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium-low and let it simmer until the lentils are soft and cooked through, about 20-25 minutes. Stir occasionally. If using a pressure cooker, cook for about 2-3 whistles.

  • 03. Prepare Tadka

    In a separate pan, heat the ghee or oil over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter. Add a pinch of asafoetida (hing).

  • 04. Combine and Simmer

    Pour the prepared tempering into the cooked lentils. Mix well. Simmer the dal for another 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Adjust the consistency by adding more water if necessary.

  • 05. Garnish and Serve

    Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with steamed rice or Indian bread (roti or naan).
