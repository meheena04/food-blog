Fish curry

Fish Curry

May 21, 2024

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 30 mins

Yields: 8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 01. Marination

    Clean the fish fillets and marinate with turmeric powder, lemon juice, and salt. Set aside for 15-20 minutes.

  • 02.Prepare the Curry Base:

    NHeat coconut oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add curry leaves and green chilies, sauté for a few seconds.

  • 03.Saute the Aromatics

    Add sliced onions and sauté until they become soft and translucent. Add julienned ginger and sliced garlic, sauté until the raw smell disappears.

  • 04.Cook the Tomatoes

    Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.

  • 05.Add Coconut Milk

    Pour in the thin coconut milk and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Add black pepper and garam masala (if using), and stir well.

  • 06.Cook the Fish

    Gently place the marinated fish fillets into the simmering curry. Cook until the fish is tender and cooked through (about 8-10 minutes). Turn the fish pieces carefully to ensure they cook evenly without breaking.

  • 07.Finish with Thick Coconut Milk

    Add the thick coconut milk and gently stir. Let it simmer for another 2-3 minutes. Adjust the consistency with a little water if needed. Check seasoning and add more salt if required.

  • 08.Serve

    Remove from heat and let the curry rest for a few minutes. Serve hot with appam, idiyappam, or steamed rice.
